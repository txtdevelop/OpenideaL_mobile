DALIPro-Control Mobile App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/09/2016

The OSRAM GmbH is pleased that you decided to use of DALIPro-Control mobile app. To protect your personal data is important to us. Therefore, we conduct our business in compliance with applicable laws on protection of personal data and data security. Below, find out what information within the DALIPro-Control application are optionally collected and how it is used.

Collection, processing and use of personal data

To use the DALIPro-Control mobile app you have to log in with your name and company name.

The use of DALIPro-Control mobile app requires an active Bluetooth connection.

Assigning a password to use the sensor

You have the option to set a password for the operation of OSRAM DALIPro-Control products. By assigning the password, the group of users of an OSRAM DALIPro-Control product can be restricted. This password is stored in the DALIPro-Control mobile app.

Please note that the password is stored in the respective device and optionally can be read by the operator or installer of the sensor or other third parties with access to the device.

Therefore, please do not use passwords that you use in other applications or elsewhere.

Changes

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. In this case, we will inform you of the change within the LUXexe Mobile App.
